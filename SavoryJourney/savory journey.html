Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Ingrediants: broccoli, olive oil, garlic, cheese, Salt and pepper

Procedure: reheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Toss broccoli florets with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes. Remove from oven, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, and toss to coat. Serve warm
